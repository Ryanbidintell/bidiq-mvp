## RECOVERY PLAYBOOK — WHEN THINGS GO SIDEWAYS

### "Nobody's responding to my outreach"
- Check spam folder + Gmail Promotions tab — your emails may not be reaching them
- Re-send via LinkedIn DM (different channel, same prospect)
- Try a totally different hook in the second send (don't just resend the same email)
- After 7 days no response: STOP. Try again in 60 days. Don't burn the relationship.

### "I'm getting calls but not converting"
- Re-read `FOUNDER_BRIEFING_TO_SELF` before next call — the most likely cause is you're pitching mid-call
- Listen back to a Granola transcript — count how many times you mentioned BidIntell. If >0, that's the problem.
- Tighten the memo voice — re-read `SAMPLE_MEMO_REFERENCE` and check yours against it

### "Memos are taking too long to write"
- Your editing instinct is too perfectionist. The first draft from Claude is usually 80% there.
- Cap memo time at 30 min after call 5. If you're going over, ship and move on.
- Compare to the sample — yours should be roughly the same length and structure

### "I'm running out of warm contacts"
- This is the cue to scale into LinkedIn cold-warm DMs (Script 4) and the public posts
- Also: ask each diagnostic recipient at the END of the follow-up email if they know one other sub owner you should talk to. ~30% will refer.

### "Calendar is full and I can't keep up"
- Throttle the inbound. Take landing page CTA down or replace with "Diagnostics paused this month — email ryan@bidintell.ai for the next opening."
- Quality > quantity. A late memo is worse than no memo.

### "A prospect ghosted after the memo"
- Send the 14-day nudge from file 06 ONCE. Then stop.
- Mark them "deferred" in tracker. Try again in 90 days only with a genuine new hook.

## END-OF-MONTH RITUAL

Last Friday of every month, sit with the tracker for 60 minutes:

1. **Review every closed-lost reason.** What's the pattern? Is it pricing? Timing? Wrong ICP? The patterns will tell you what to fix next month.

2. **Read 3 random memos from the month.** Are they getting better or staying the same? Voice tightening? More specific?

3. **Update the patterns doc.** What's the most common regret pattern across the month's diagnostics? What's the most common wish?

4. **Pull one quote per month** for content. Anonymized. Use it the following month in a LinkedIn post.

5. **Recalibrate target list.** Who's the next batch of warm contacts? Who needs re-engagement at 60+ days?

The compounding value of this motion is in this monthly ritual. The diagnostics produce data. The data produces strategy. The strategy improves the diagnostics. Don't skip the ritual.
