# 03 — CALENDLY INTAKE SETUP

**Purpose:** Make booking the diagnostic frictionless, capture intake data, screen for fit.
**Time to set up:** 20 minutes, one-time.

---

## EVENT CONFIGURATION

**Event Name:** BidIntell Bid Selection Diagnostic — 30 min

**Event Description (shown to bookers):**

> A 30-minute structured conversation walking through your last 10 bids — wins, losses, and the ones you regret chasing. Afterward I send you a written one-page read on the patterns I see in your bid selection.
>
> No product walkthrough. No pitch on the call. The deliverable is the memo.
>
> Built for owners and presidents of specialty subcontractors, $2M–$20M revenue, doing commercial work.

**Duration:** 30 minutes
**Location:** Zoom (auto-generated link)
**Buffer time:** 15 min before, 15 min after (you need note-taking time after each call)
**Daily cap:** 2 calls per day max
**Weekly cap:** 5 calls per week
**Available days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday only (protect Monday and Friday for build/strategy/personal)
**Available hours:** 9 AM – 4 PM Central (no early morning, no end-of-day fatigue calls)
**Minimum scheduling notice:** 24 hours
**Maximum scheduling window:** 21 days out

---

## INTAKE FORM QUESTIONS

These are the **4 questions** you ask on the booking page. Mark all as required.

**Question 1: Company name and your role**
- Type: Short text
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Owner, President, VP, etc."

**Question 2: Trade or scope you specialize in**
- Type: Short text
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Division 10, flooring, insulation, MEP, glazing, etc."

**Question 3: Approximate annual revenue**
- Type: Single-select dropdown
- Required: Yes
- Options:
  - Under $2M
  - $2M – $5M
  - $5M – $10M
  - $10M – $20M
  - $20M – $50M
  - Over $50M

**Question 4: One sentence on what you're hoping to get out of this**
- Type: Long text (paragraph)
- Required: Yes
- Help text: "Honestly, whatever's on your mind. This helps me show up prepared."

**Why these 4 specifically:** Q1-3 confirm ICP fit before the call. Q4 is the diagnostic before the diagnostic — it tells you what they're actually thinking about, which lets you steer the call toward their real concern in question 5 of the script.

## RESCHEDULING POLICY

Allow rescheduling up to 4 hours before. After that, force a manual reach-out. (You don't want last-minute auto-reschedules eating your prep time.)

---

## CANCELLATION HANDLING

If they cancel without rescheduling, send this 24 hours later:

> Hey [Name] — saw the cancel. No problem at all. Want me to leave the diagnostic offer open for whenever you've got time, or is this a "not now"? Either answer is fine.
>
> Ryan

If they say "not now," log them in the tracking sheet under "Deferred." Try again in 60 days.

## INTEGRATIONS TO TURN ON

1. **Google Calendar** (or Outlook) — auto-block time
2. **Zoom** — auto-generate meeting links
3. **Postmark or Gmail** — confirmation/reminder emails
4. **Webhook to your tracking sheet** (advanced — set up later, not week 1):
   - When booking happens, push intake form answers + booking time to a Google Sheet row

You can do the webhook with Zapier (free tier) or Make.com — but **don't bother with this in week 1**. Manual entry into the tracking sheet for the first 5-10 prospects is fine and gives you better field discipline anyway.

---

## SCREENING — WHEN TO REJECT A BOOKING

Most bookings are good. Reject only if:

- Revenue is "Under $2M" or "Over $50M" — wrong ICP, and the diagnostic won't produce useful patterns for them
- They're a GC, CM, or owner — wrong side of the table
- They list themselves as "Estimator" or "PM" without ownership authority — they can't make the buying decision

If you need to reject, send this:

> Hey [Name] — thanks for booking. Quick honest note: the diagnostic is built specifically for owners/presidents at specialty subs in the $2M–$20M range, and based on your intake answers it sounds like the patterns I'd surface won't quite fit your situation.
>
> I don't want to take 30 minutes of your time on something that won't produce a useful memo.
>
> If your context changes — different role, different size shop — open invite to come back. Genuinely.
>
> Ryan

Cancel the booking after sending. **Do not run a diagnostic with someone outside ICP.** It pollutes your dataset and produces a memo that won't be useful, which damages the offer's reputation.
