Scoring model

Four factors. One score.

The BidIndex Score is a fit score, not a win probability. It tells you how well a bid matches your specific business.

Factor 1
Location Fit
Distance from your office to the job site. Weighted by your service area settings.
Factor 2
Trade Match
How well the spec sections and scope match your CSI trades. Section-level, not just division.
Factor 3
Client Relationship
Your star rating and win history with the GC, plus competitive pressure from past bids.
Factor 4
Keywords and Contract Risk
Good and bad keyword scan plus automatic detection of pay-if-paid, retainage, and liquidated damages clauses.
