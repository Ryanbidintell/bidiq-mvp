## What BidIntell Actually Does

Pricing only makes sense once the product does.

[BidIntell](https://bidintell.ai/) is a pre-bid intelligence platform for commercial subcontractors — mechanical, electrical, plumbing, drywall, painting, flooring. If you receive bid invites and have to decide which ones are worth opening, this is what it's built for.

You upload a bid PDF or forward an invite to your personal BidIntell address. The platform reads it, pulls out scope, project details, and contract terms, then generates a BidIndex Score from 0 to 100. That score is built around your trade, your service area, your client history, and your current capacity — not a generic ranking every sub in your market sees. You get a GO, REVIEW, or PASS before your estimator opens a single plan.

Contract risks get flagged automatically: pay-if-paid clauses, retainage terms, vague scope language, aggressive timelines. And every outcome you record — Won, Lost, Ghosted, Passed, No Bid — feeds a learning engine that sharpens future scores.

That's the product. Now the pricing.

## What's Included at Every Tier

Specific plan pricing isn't listed publicly, but BidIntell's core feature set runs across the platform. Here's what you're working with:

### BidIndex Score (0–100)

A personalized fit score for each bid — not a shared ranking every sub in your trade receives. Weighted by your trade, service area, client relationships, and current capacity. You can also adjust the scoring weights yourself using presets like Relationship-First or Risk-Averse, or configure your own priorities.

### PDF and Email Ingestion

Upload a PDF or forward an invite to your personal BidIntell address. Scope, project details, and contract terms are extracted automatically. No manual entry.

### Contract Risk Detection

Before your estimator touches the plans, BidIntell flags specific risk clauses: pay-if-paid, retainage terms, vague scope language, and aggressive timelines. Catching these early saves real time — and avoids the back-and-forth with legal or project management that usually happens too late.

### Outcome Tracking and Learning Loop

Record every result: Won, Lost, Ghosted, Passed, No Bid. Add decline reasons. Over time, the platform surfaces win patterns by project type, client, and geography. Win patterns don't show up in your gut. They show up in your data.

### Competitive Pressure Score

Built from anonymized data across all platform users. Shows how many subs typically bid a given client by trade. If seven or eight electrical subs routinely chase a particular GC's work, you'll know before you spend time pricing it.

### Client Reputation Tracking

Response rates, ghost rates, and repeat-partner signals drawn from collective bid history across the platform. Useful for knowing which GCs are worth your time and which ones are just collecting numbers.

## FAQs

**Does BidIntell publish its subscription pricing?**
No. Specific plan pricing isn't listed on the website. You can access pricing details after starting the 7-day free trial or by scheduling a demo at bidintell.ai.

**Is there really no credit card required for the free trial?**
Correct. The 7-day free trial requires no credit card at signup. You can score real bids and use the full platform before any payment information is needed.

**How long does setup take?**
About 5 minutes. You configure your trade, service area, and scoring preferences, then you're ready to upload your first bid.

**What does BidIntell score, exactly?**
BidIntell generates a BidIndex Score from 0 to 100 for each bid, based on your trade, service area, client history, and current capacity. That score produces a GO, REVIEW, or PASS recommendation before estimating begins.

**Does BidIntell work for trades other than MEP?**
Yes. It's built for mechanical, electrical, and plumbing subcontractors as well as drywall, painting, and flooring trades. Building material distributors and manufacturers' reps quoting on commercial projects are also supported.

**How is BidIntell different from a bid board like PlanHub or ConstructConnect?**
Bid boards find you more projects to bid. BidIntell scores the projects already in your inbox. It doesn't source new opportunities — it helps you decide which existing invites are worth your estimators' time, flags contract risks, and gets sharper the more outcomes you record.

**What happens to my data on the platform?**
BidIntell uses anonymized, aggregated data from all platform users to power features like the Competitive Pressure Score and client reputation tracking. Individual bid data is never shared. Only anonymized patterns surface across the platform.
