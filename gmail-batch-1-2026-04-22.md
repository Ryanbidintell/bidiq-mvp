## How to use

1. Open Gmail compose (or click each lead's email link below to pre-fill)
2. Copy **TO** → paste in To field
3. Copy **SUBJECT** → paste in subject line
4. Copy **BODY** (everything between the `---BODY---` markers) → paste in body

**Pacing:** Send all 25 over the morning (e.g. 8am–11am) — too fast and Gmail throttles. Aim for 1 every 5–10 min.
