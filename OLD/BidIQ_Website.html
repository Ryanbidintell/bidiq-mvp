The BidIQ Story

BidIQ was born from frustration. After 10 years running a commercial HVAC contracting business, founder Ryan had wasted thousands of hours bidding on projects he never had a chance at. GCs who ghosted. Bids with 15 competitors. Contracts with impossible terms.

The tools that existed ‚Äî plan rooms, takeoff software, estimating platforms ‚Äî all focused on DOING the bid faster. None of them answered the fundamental question: Should I even bid this in the first place?

BidIQ is the answer. An AI-powered intelligence layer that analyzes bid documents and delivers personalized GO/NO-GO recommendations based on each contractor's unique business model.
