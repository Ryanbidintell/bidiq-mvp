Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

BidIntell, LLC ("BidIntell," "we," "us," or "our") operates the BidIntell platform at bidintell.ai. 1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, company name, office location (city and state), and the trades or product lines your company provides. This information is necessary to deliver personalized scoring.

1.2 Business Preferences

During onboarding, you provide business parameters including service area radius, project size preferences, risk tolerance, current capacity status, and score component weights. These preferences are used solely to personalize your BidIndex Score and recommendations.

1.3 Bid Documents

When you upload bid documents (PDFs, specifications, drawings), we process them using AI to extract project details for scoring. Your uploaded documents are stored securely and are never shared with other users, general contractors, or third parties.

1.4 Bid Decision Data

We collect the outcomes you record (won, lost, ghosted, declined), decline reasons, decision confidence ratings, competitor information, and GC responsiveness feedback. In future phases, this data may be aggregated and anonymized to power market-level intelligence ‚Äî but your individual decisions and business details are never exposed.

When you add or rate general contractors, including star ratings and risk tags, this information is stored as your personal intelligence. Custom risk tags may be reviewed by our team and, if broadly useful, promoted to a shared tag list ‚Äî but your identity is never attached to tags visible to other users.

1.6 Usage Data

We automatically collect standard technical information including IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and feature usage patterns. This data helps us improve the platform and diagnose technical issues.

1.7 Feedback Data

If you submit feedback through our in-app feedback widget, we collect the feedback type, message content, the page you were on, and any screenshots you choose to attach.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Personalized Scoring: Your business preferences power the BidIndex Score algorithm, ensuring recommendations reflect your specific situation ‚Äî your territory, your trades, your GC relationships, and your risk tolerance.
  • AI Document Analysis: Uploaded bid documents are processed through AI (Anthropic's Claude API) to extract project details, detect contract risk clauses, and match trade scope. Document content is sent to the AI provider for processing only and is not used to train AI models.
  • Platform Improvement: Anonymized, aggregated usage patterns help us improve scoring accuracy, identify bugs, and prioritize features.
  • Communications: We send transactional emails related to your account (bid analyses, notifications, password resets). We do not send marketing emails without your consent.
  • Future Market Intelligence (Phase 3+): In future versions, anonymized and aggregated decision data may power market-level indices (such as bid participation trends or GC responsiveness patterns). Individual data is never identifiable in these outputs. We enforce minimum sample thresholds before reporting any aggregated data.

3. How We Protect Your Data

Your bid documents and business data are sensitive. We treat them accordingly:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest using AES-256 encryption via our infrastructure provider (Supabase).
  • Access Controls: Your data is isolated to your account. No other user can see your documents, scores, outcomes, or GC ratings.
  • AI Processing: Bid documents sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis are governed by Anthropic's data usage policies, which do not use customer inputs for model training.
  • No Data Sales: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or business data to third parties. Ever.
  • Employee Access: Internal access to user data is limited to essential operations (support, debugging) and is logged.
