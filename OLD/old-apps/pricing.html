Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "bid analysis"?

Each time you upload and analyze a new set of bid documents, that counts as one analysis. You can view, edit, re-analyze, and chat about analyzed bids unlimited times without using additional credits.

Is the AI advisor chat really unlimited?

Yes! Every plan includes unlimited conversations with your personalized AI advisor. Ask questions, get strategy advice, and understand your scores â€” no limits, no extra charges.

What happens if I go over my monthly bid limit?

No problem! Starter pays $3 per additional bid, and Professional pays $2 per bid. Overages are charged automatically at the end of your billing cycle. If you consistently need more, we'll recommend upgrading to save money.

What's included in the beta?

The first 20 beta users get FREE access for 60 days (all features, unlimited bids). After beta, you'll lock in a special early adopter rate of $39/month forever. No credit card required to join beta.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access through the end of your billing period.

Do unused bids roll over?

No, bid limits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. This keeps pricing simple and predictable. If you consistently need more bids, upgrade to Professional or Team for better per-bid pricing.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're SOC 2 Type II compliant and follow industry best practices for data security. Your bid documents and business data are never shared or used for training AI models.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change plans anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle, and you'll receive a prorated credit for the difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and ACH bank transfers for Enterprise customers.
